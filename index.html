Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need from me?

+
To create your perfect thumbnail, I'll need: 1) The video title or main text you want featured, 2) Your preferred style or reference images, 3) Any specific characters, skins, or elements you want included, 4) Your channel branding colors (if applicable), and 5) Any additional details or special requests. The more information you provide, the better I can bring your vision to life!

Do you offer refunds?

+
No refunds are issued once design work has begun. However, I'm committed to your satisfaction and will work with you through revisions to ensure you're happy with the final product. If there's an issue before work starts, we can discuss options on a case-by-case basis.

Can you match my channel's branding?

+
Absolutely! I specialize in creating cohesive thumbnail designs that align with your existing channel branding. Just provide me with your brand colors, fonts, logo, or examples of your current style, and I'll ensure every thumbnail fits perfectly with your channel's aesthetic.

Do you work with other games besides Fortnite?

+
While I specialize in Fortnite thumbnails, I'm experienced with other games too! If you need thumbnails for different games, feel free to reach out and we can discuss your specific needs. My design skills translate well across different gaming content.

What's the difference between Basic and Advanced thumbnails?

+
Basic thumbnails (¬£1.50) feature clean designs with standard text and simple effects - perfect for consistent, professional uploads. Advanced thumbnails (¬£3.00) include premium lighting effects, complex text styling, custom backgrounds, and more intricate compositions. Advanced designs typically have more visual impact and detail work.

How long does delivery take?

+
Standard delivery is typically 24-48 hours after payment confirmation. For rush orders, I offer a Fast Delivery option (+¬£1.00) which guarantees your thumbnail within 12 hours. Bulk orders (5+ thumbnails) may take 3-5 days depending on complexity.

What file formats do you provide?

+
All thumbnails are delivered as high-resolution PNG files (1920x1080px) optimized for YouTube. If you need different formats (JPG, PSD source files, or custom dimensions), just let me know and I can accommodate most requests at no extra charge!

How many revisions are included?

+
Each thumbnail includes up to 2 free revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Minor tweaks like text changes or color adjustments are always free. Major redesigns may require an additional fee, but I'll always work with you to get it right!
